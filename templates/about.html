About the Project

This data visualization project was created for HackRice XI by Baylee Keevan, Sophia Rohlfsen, Cyrus Najmi-Vannini, and Ethan Pham. Our data science project focuses on environmental justice, highlighting the economic inequities of clean air.

The Air Quality Index data was collected from the set Tables of Daily AQI provided by the EPA, and the median income data was from the data set Unemployment and median household income for the U.S., States, and counties, 2000-20 provided by the USDA.
